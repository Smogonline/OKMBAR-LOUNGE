About Us

Your Ultimate Escape In Abeokuta

OKM Bar & Lounge is Abeokuta's go-to destination for great drinks, exciting games, and a relaxing atmosphere. Nestled at Anobe Complex in the heart of Somorin Obantoko, we offer an experience that blends the vibrancy of a premium bar with the comfort of a modern lounge.

Whether you're winding down after a long day, hosting a gathering, or looking for a venue with reliable power and security — we've got you covered.
